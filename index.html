WHO WE ARE? AMB IT Solutions is an Information Technology services provider; it humbly started as an Internet café back in 2011 and later advanced to an IT solutions provider that caters Small-Office-Home-Office (SOHO) clients and small and medium sized Businesses (SMB). The company provides professional IT services and products. We believe that providing “quality” IT services should not be expensive but rather cost-effective. In AMB IT Solutions, we focus on providing reliable and cost-effective value-added services such as IT consulting, and other IT related services to our clients, wherein majority are non-profit organizations and small companies that require quality IT products and services.
